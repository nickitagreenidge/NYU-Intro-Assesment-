Spoony

07/08/1992

This is a fun tribute page to our loyal companions!

Spoony was the most cuddly pet ever with orange and white fur. I fell in love with spoony when he tickled me under the table and made me fall out of my chair.

  1. He enjoyed the best of Barbadian cuisine.
  2. He played with all the other pets.
  3. He loved to chase me around.
